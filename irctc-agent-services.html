What is an IRCTC Authorized Agent?

An IRCTC Authorized Agent is an individual or business officially certified by the Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC) to book train e-tickets for passengers. Unlike personal user IDs, an official IRCTC Agent ID allows you to:

  • Book an unlimited number of train tickets legally.
  • Access dedicated agent portals with enhanced features (e.g., IRCTC agent login, special quotas).
  • Offer value-added services related to train travel.
  • Operate a recognized and legitimate railway ticket booking business.

Eligibility: Who Can Become an IRCTC Agent?

The eligibility criteria are generally straightforward:

  • You must be an Indian citizen.
  • Minimum age requirement (e.g., 18 years).
  • Basic computer and internet knowledge.
  • Valid PAN Card and Aadhaar Card.
  • A valid email ID and mobile number.
  • Proof of address for your shop/office or residence.
