What Is an IRCTC Agent?

An IRCTC agent is officially appointed and authorized to book railway e-tickets using a special agent login provided by IRCTC. Unlike regular users, agents can book unlimited tickets daily, offer customer support, and access additional business tools.

  4. Pay the one-time registration and service fee
