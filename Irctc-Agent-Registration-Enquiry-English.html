Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is an IRCTC Agent Login?

Selling tickets with a personal ID is illegal according to IRCTC's business rules. Agent login is a business login designed by IRCTC to provide a special login for ticket booking. People involved in the travel business or running a train booking shop can obtain an IRCTC license for e-ticketing. There are two types of IRCTC agent logins: B2B (bookings are made on the Principal Service Provider's portal) and ICS (bookings are made on the IRCTC portal).

How to become an Authorized IRCTC Agent online?

Anyone can become an IRCTC agent in three easy steps: 1. Fill out the online form. 2. Submit documents. 3. Pay the registration fee and obtain the license. With Ftech Global Solutions, this process is even easier.

What are the benefits of being an IRCTC agent?

Agents can book an unlimited number of IRCTC e-tickets. Agents can book IRCTC tour packages. Agents can book flights and hotels from IRCTC. Issue authorized tickets without fear of cancellation. IRCTC agents can book General, Tatkal, Waiting List, RAC tickets. The IRCTC agent commission is ₹20/40 per PNR and up to 1% PG charges. The agent's name will be displayed on the IRCTC website. No trade license is required for a railway agency. Your agency's details will be printed on the ticket. IRCTC agents get access to a web portal for faster ticket booking. Railway ticket fare is deducted from your wallet, so tickets are generated faster.

How much can an IRCTC agent earn?

IRCTC agents can earn ₹85,000 or more per month, depending on the number of railway tickets and other travel bookings they make in a month. With Ftech Global Solutions, you can maximize your earning potential.

What is the Tatkal booking time for an IRCTC agent?

IRCTC agents can book Tatkal tickets, but under new rules from July 1, 2025, they will be restricted from Tatkal booking for AC class from 10:00 AM to 10:30 AM and for Non-AC class from 11:00 AM to 11:30 AM. Agents can book only after this window closes. From July 1, 2025, Aadhaar authentication and from July 15, 2025, OTP verification will be mandatory for all Tatkal bookings.

What are the booking hours for agents?

From 12:20 AM to 11:45 PM.

What is the maximum number of bookings allowed per month?

IRCTC agents can book an unlimited number of e-tickets.

Can I use the login on any machine?

Yes, agents can book IRCTC tickets on any mobile/laptop or desktop using OTP-based login.

What is the validity period of an agent account?

The IRCTC agent account is renewed every year.

What is the validity period of the Digital Certificate?

The validity of the Digital Certificate is 2 years.

In how many days can I become an IRCTC agent?

Typically, it takes less than 3 working days, plus the time it takes for the dongle to reach your location. OTP-based login accounts are activated on the same day.

Can I book tickets from my mobile phone?

Yes, using OTP-based login, you can book IRCTC tickets from your mobile phone.

Will I get training for ticket booking?

Yes, the support team will provide online training via remote desk. Ftech Global Solutions offers comprehensive training and continuous support to its agents.

What other services can I get with IRCTC registration?

With an IRCTC agency, agents can book flight tickets and bus tickets, mobile and DTH recharges, money transfers, Dubai visas, IRCTC international tour packages, IRCTC hotels, insurance, etc. Through Ftech Global Solutions, you can provide all these services on a single platform, increasing your earning opportunities.

Is GST registration required to become an IRCTC agent?

No, GST registration is not mandatory for an IRCTC agent.

My PAN is already registered with IRCTC, how can I release it?

You can release the PAN by visiting 'My Account' > 'My Profile' > 'Release PAN/Aadhaar' on the IRCTC website.

Can I use a mobile number already registered with IRCTC?

No, a new mobile number is required that is not registered with IRCTC for personal use.

Can I use an email ID already registered with IRCTC?

No, a new email ID is required that is not registered with IRCTC for personal use.

What address proof can I provide?

Any government document can be accepted, such as electricity bill, water bill, Aadhaar card, Voter ID card, PSU bank passbook, DL, rental agreement, landline phone bill, etc.

Does my business need to be registered?

There is no requirement for business registration; you can run the business in your name with some trade name. However, it is helpful if you register your business with a local government body.

Who is a PSP (Principal Service Provider)?

Since IRCTC does not directly appoint agents, it has authorized certain agencies known as PSPs. PSPs handle the necessary formalities for IRCTC agent appointment. All agents created by a PSP recharge their wallets through the PSP. The PSP will charge a fixed processing fee from the agent. Ftech Global Solutions is an authorized distributor that provides these services to you from IRCTC's authorized PSPs.

Can I directly become an agent through the IRCTC website?

No, only a PSP can provide IRCTC agency. Authorized distributors like Ftech Global Solutions facilitate registration through PSPs.

Is a bank account necessary?

No, payment can be made through the RDS wallet provided by your PSP.

What is an RDS wallet?

IRCTC provides its agents with a Rolling Deposit Scheme (RDS) facility for quick ticket booking. To avail of the RDS facility, agents can deposit the desired amount into the PSP's bank account. Agents can purchase tickets against the money deposited in the RDS wallet. RDS reduces dependence on the bank for OTP and PIN, making it a very fast and secure process. The RDS wallet can only be used through a transaction password, which the PSP will provide.

What are the benefits of using an RDS wallet?

Payments made for bookings using the RDS wallet are faster. Payment gateway charges are printed on the ticket only if payment is made using the RDS wallet. If payment is made through any other mode, PG charges will not be printed on the ticket, meaning they cannot be charged to the customer. It is advisable for agents to use the RDS wallet for their benefit. PSP convenience fees will apply in both cases.

How do I recharge my IRCTC agent wallet?

The wallet can be recharged via UPI/IMPS (recommended), NEFT, challan, and direct cash deposit into the PSP's bank account.

How is payment made for ticket bookings?

Agents can pay for rail tickets via RDS wallet, internet banking, debit card, credit card, mobile wallet, etc.

How can I cancel tickets?

Cancellation can be done from the booking history page; agents need to select the passengers before clicking the cancel button.

How do I file a TDR?

There are two ways to file a TDR: 1. Email the PNR number to agenttdr@irctc.co.in. 2. Report to your PSP for TDR.

What is the difference between personal login booking and agent login booking?

Personal login can only be used for personal or family rail tickets; selling rail tickets on a personal ID is an offense under Section 143 of the Railway Act. A user can book only 6 tickets per month (12 after PAN and Aadhaar verification). Agent login is a special login designed for commercial use. Agents can book unlimited tickets and charge ₹20 for Sleeper and ₹40 for AC class, along with up to 1% payment gateway charges, respectively.

Where can I see the list of Principal Service Providers?

You can view the list of Principal Service Providers on the official IRCTC website.

What should I check before selecting a PSP?

Choose a PSP that provides the best support service. Look for timely and fast wallet credits, free wallet credits, chat, phone and email support, timely ticket refunds, and accurate and transparent reporting of transactions. Ftech Global Solutions meets all these criteria and provides you with a reliable partner.

Can I run my business from home?

Certainly, agents need to share any valid address proof of their workplace; an Aadhaar card is sufficient if working from home.

What happens after filling the registration form?

After filling the registration form, Ftech Global Solutions personnel will call you for the next process. The further process includes: 1. Share documents. 2. Pay a registration fee of ₹3,999. 3. After eKYC and other formalities, obtain the IRCTC agency within a week.

Is the registration fee refundable?

No, the registration fee is non-refundable; it will be forfeited if the agent withdraws their registration.

What is Digital Certificate (DSC)/Dongle for an IRCTC Agent?

A DSC or dongle is similar to a USB pen drive; it is not an internet dongle. Without a DSC, agents cannot log in to IRCTC. The login page will only open if the IRCTC website detects the agent's DSC. The PSP will provide you with the Digital Certificate (DSC).

How long does it take to get the dongle?

The entire process will take 5-7 working days after payment. The PSP will provide you with the DSC/dongle via courier to your office/shop/home address. Once you receive the DSC, you will get your user ID and password immediately after uploading money to the RDS wallet.

Can I receive the dongle at a different address if I am not available at the registered address?

Yes, the dongle can be received anywhere within India.

What is eKYC and video processing for IRCTC Agent?

eKYC and video processing are part of DSC and are required to create the DSC. Agents will have to share an OTP and make a video before receiving the dongle/DSC. DSC is required for the agent to log in to the IRCTC website.

How can I cancel my registration?

Registration can be canceled anytime before payment.

When can a TDR be filed?

Please refer to the official IRCTC website for TDR rules.

What equipment is required for an IRCTC Agent?

1. Computer/Laptop 2. Printer 3. Internet.

How much amount is deducted on ticket cancellation?

Please refer to the official IRCTC website for ticket cancellation charges.

Can concessional tickets be booked from agent login?

Yes, passengers will get all the benefits provided by IRCTC on tickets booked by agents.

How to locate tickets?

Go to My Account - My Transactions - Booked Ticket History.

Do agents have a separate quota for seat booking?

No, IRCTC does not provide any separate quota to agents.

Do agents get priority in seat booking?

IRCTC agents do not get any priority; the booking process is the same for everyone.

Can I book Tatkal tickets or Premium Tatkal tickets?

Agents are only allowed to book Tatkal tickets. However, from July 1, 2025, agents will be restricted from Tatkal booking for AC class from 10:00 AM to 10:30 AM and for Non-AC class from 11:00 AM to 11:30 AM. Agents can book only after this window closes. Premium Tatkal tickets are not allowed for agents to book.

Are senior citizen and other discounts provided on tickets booked by agents?

Yes, passengers will receive all benefits on tickets booked by IRCTC agents.

How many days in advance can I book tickets?

Tickets can be booked 150 days in advance of travel.

How much refund can I get on confirmed Tatkal tickets?

No refund is provided on confirmed Tatkal tickets.

What are the pros and cons of being an IRCTC ticket agent?

Pros: Agents can book an unlimited number of IRCTC e-tickets. The IRCTC agent commission is ₹20/40 per PNR and up to 1% PG charges. The agent's name will be displayed on the IRCTC website. Your agency's details will be printed on the ticket. Railway ticket fare is deducted from your wallet, so tickets are generated faster. Agents can book IRCTC tour packages. Cons: Agents are not allowed to book Tatkal tickets for AC and Sleeper class from 10:00 AM to 10:30 AM and 11:00 AM to 11:30 AM respectively, whereas general users can book Tatkal tickets at 10:00 AM and 11:00 AM. Agents are completely dependent on their PSPs; they cannot directly contact IRCTC.

What type of computer is sufficient for an IRCTC ticket agent?

For the travel business alone, any low-configuration computer is sufficient, but with high-speed internet. Any laptop or computer with the latest Windows and hardware can be used for ticketing (i3 processor and 4 GB RAM recommended).

Which operating system can be used for IRCTC booking?

Only Windows operating system is required for IRCTC booking; Apple operating system is not suitable for IRCTC. However, an Apple computer or laptop with Windows installed can be used for IRCTC ticket booking.
