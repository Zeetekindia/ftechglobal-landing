Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) about Becoming an IRCTC Agent

What is an IRCTC Agent Login?

Selling tickets with a personal ID is illegal according to IRCTC's business rules. An **IRCTC agent login** is a business login designed by IRCTC to provide a special login for ticket booking. People involved in the travel business or running a train booking shop can obtain an **IRCTC license** for e-ticketing. There are two types of IRCTC agent logins: B2B (bookings are made on the Principal Service Provider's portal) and ICS (bookings are made on the IRCTC portal).

How to become an Authorized IRCTC Agent online?

Anyone can **become an IRCTC agent** in three easy steps: 1. Fill out the online registration form. 2. Submit required documents. 3. Pay the registration fee and obtain your **IRCTC agent ID** and license. With Ftech Global Solutions, this process is even easier and designed for a smooth **IRCTC agent registration**.

What are the benefits of being an IRCTC agent?

**IRCTC agents** can book an unlimited number of **IRCTC e-tickets**. Agents can book IRCTC tour packages. Agents can book flights and hotels directly from IRCTC. You can issue authorized tickets without fear of cancellation, ensuring a legitimate **travel business**. **IRCTC agents** can book General, Tatkal, Waiting List, and RAC tickets. The **IRCTC agent commission** is ₹20/40 per PNR and up to 1% PG charges. Your agency's name will be displayed on the IRCTC website and your agency's details will be printed on the ticket. No trade license is strictly required for a railway agency. **IRCTC agents** get access to a dedicated web portal for faster ticket booking, and railway ticket fare is deducted from your wallet, making ticket generation swift.

How much can an IRCTC agent earn?

**IRCTC agents** can earn ₹85,000 or more per month, depending on the number of **railway tickets** and other travel bookings they make in a month. Ftech Global Solutions is dedicated to helping you maximize your **earning potential** as an **IRCTC authorized agent**.

What is the Tatkal booking time for an IRCTC agent?

**IRCTC agents** can book **Tatkal tickets**, but under new rules from July 1, 2025, they will be restricted from Tatkal booking for AC class from 10:00 AM to 10:30 AM and for Non-AC class from 11:00 AM to 11:30 AM. Agents can book only after this window closes. From July 1, 2025, Aadhaar authentication and from July 15, 2025, OTP verification will be mandatory for all Tatkal bookings, affecting **IRCTC agent Tatkal bookings**.

What are the booking hours for agents?

The general **IRCTC agent booking hours** are from 12:20 AM to 11:45 PM.

What is the maximum number of bookings allowed per month?

**IRCTC agents** can book an **unlimited number of e-tickets**, offering significant business potential.

Can I use the login on any machine?

Yes, agents can book **IRCTC tickets** on any mobile/laptop or desktop using OTP-based login, providing flexibility for your **online travel business**.

What is the validity period of an agent account?

The **IRCTC agent account** is renewed every year, ensuring continued operation of your **IRCTC agency**.

What is the validity period of the Digital Certificate?

The validity of the **Digital Certificate (DSC)** for **IRCTC agents** is 2 years.

Is GST registration required to become an IRCTC agent?

No, **GST registration** is not mandatory for an **IRCTC agent**.

My PAN is already registered with IRCTC, how can I release it?

You can release your **PAN** by visiting 'My Account' > 'My Profile' > 'Release PAN/Aadhaar' on the official **IRCTC website**.

Can I use a mobile number already registered with IRCTC?

No, a new mobile number is required that is not registered with IRCTC for personal use. This is crucial for your **IRCTC agent ID** setup and maintaining compliance.

Can I use an email ID already registered with IRCTC?

No, a new email ID is required that is not registered with IRCTC for personal use. This ensures a dedicated email for your **IRCTC agent operations** and communications.

What address proof can I provide?

Any valid government document can be accepted as address proof for **IRCTC agent registration**, such as electricity bill, water bill, Aadhaar card, Voter ID card, PSU bank passbook, DL, rental agreement, landline phone bill, etc.

Does my business need to be registered?

There is no strict requirement for formal business registration to **become an IRCTC agent**; you can run the business in your name with a suitable trade name. However, if you register your **travel business** with a local government body, it can be beneficial for various reasons.

Who is a PSP (Principal Service Provider)?

Since IRCTC does not directly appoint individual agents, it has authorized certain agencies known as PSPs. These **Principal Service Providers** handle the necessary formalities for **IRCTC agent appointment**. All agents created by a PSP recharge their wallets through that PSP. The PSP will charge a fixed processing fee from the agent. Ftech Global Solutions is an authorized distributor that provides these **IRCTC agent services** to you from IRCTC's authorized PSPs, ensuring a smooth and compliant **IRCTC agent registration** process.

Can I directly become an agent through the IRCTC website?

No, only an authorized **PSP** can provide an **IRCTC agency**. Authorized distributors like Ftech Global Solutions facilitate **IRCTC agent registration** through PSPs, making your journey to **become an IRCTC agent** seamless and official.

Is a bank account necessary?

No, for **IRCTC agent bookings**, payment can be made conveniently through the **RDS wallet** provided by your PSP, reducing the need for direct bank account linking for every transaction.

What is an RDS wallet?

IRCTC provides its agents with a **Rolling Deposit Scheme (RDS)** facility for quick **ticket booking**. To avail of the RDS facility, **IRCTC agents** can deposit the desired amount into the PSP's bank account. Agents can then purchase tickets against the money deposited in the **RDS wallet**. RDS reduces dependence on the bank for OTP and PIN, making it a very fast and secure process for **IRCTC agent bookings**. The RDS wallet can only be used through a transaction password, which the PSP will provide.

What are the benefits of using an RDS wallet?

Payments made for bookings using the **RDS wallet** are significantly faster, crucial for busy **IRCTC agents**. If the **RDS wallet** is used for payment, payment gateway charges are printed on the ticket. If payment is made through any other mode, PG charges will not be printed on the ticket, meaning they cannot be charged to the customer. It is highly advisable for **IRCTC agents** to use the RDS wallet for their benefit. PSP convenience fees will apply in both cases.

How do I recharge my IRCTC agent wallet?

Your **IRCTC agent wallet** (RDS wallet) can be conveniently recharged via UPI/IMPS (recommended for speed), NEFT, challan, and direct cash deposit into the PSP's bank account.

How is payment made for ticket bookings?

**IRCTC agents** can pay for rail tickets for their customers via the **RDS wallet**, internet banking, debit card, credit card, mobile wallet, and other secure payment options.

How can I cancel tickets?

**Ticket cancellation** for **IRCTC agents** can be done from the booking history page on your agent portal; agents need to select the passengers before clicking the cancel button.

How do I file a TDR?

There are two primary ways to file a **TDR (Ticket Deposit Receipt)** as an **IRCTC agent**: 1. Email the PNR number to agenttdr@irctc.co.in. 2. Report the TDR request to your PSP for assistance.

What is the difference between personal login booking and agent login booking?

**Personal IRCTC login** can only be used for personal or family rail tickets; selling rail tickets on a personal ID is an offense under Section 143 of the Railway Act. A personal user can book only 6 tickets per month (12 after PAN and Aadhaar verification). **IRCTC agent login**, on the other hand, is a special commercial login. Agents can book **unlimited tickets** and charge a commission of ₹20 for Sleeper and ₹40 for AC class, along with up to 1% payment gateway charges, making it a legitimate **travel business** opportunity.

Where can I see the list of Principal Service Providers?

You can view the official list of **Principal Service Providers (PSPs)** authorized by IRCTC on the **IRCTC official website**.

What is Digital Certificate (DSC)/Dongle for an IRCTC Agent?

A **Digital Certificate (DSC)** or **dongle** for an **IRCTC agent** is a cryptographic USB device, similar to a pen drive, but it's not an internet dongle. Without a DSC, agents cannot log in to the IRCTC system. The login page will only open if the IRCTC website detects the agent's DSC. Your **PSP** will provide you with this essential **Digital Certificate (DSC)** as part of your **IRCTC agent toolkit**.

How long does it take to get the dongle?

The entire process for obtaining the **DSC/dongle** and activating your **IRCTC agent ID** will take approximately 5-7 working days after payment. Your PSP will provide you with the DSC/dongle via courier to your specified office/shop/home address. Once you receive the DSC, you will get your user ID and password immediately after uploading money to the **RDS wallet**, allowing you to start your **IRCTC ticket booking business** swiftly.

Can I receive the dongle at a different address if I am not available at the registered address?

Yes, the **DSC/dongle** for your **IRCTC agency** can be received anywhere within India, offering convenience and flexibility for your setup.

What is eKYC and video processing for IRCTC Agent?

**eKYC (electronic Know Your Customer)** and video processing are integral parts of obtaining your **Digital Certificate (DSC)** and are essential for its creation. As an aspiring **IRCTC agent**, you will need to share an OTP and record a video before receiving the dongle/DSC. The DSC is a mandatory requirement for the **IRCTC agent** to log in to the **IRCTC website** securely.

What equipment is required for an IRCTC Agent?

To successfully operate as an **IRCTC agent**, you will primarily need: 1. A functional **computer/laptop** 2. A **printer** for issuing tickets and other documents 3. A stable and high-speed **internet connection**.

How much amount is deducted on ticket cancellation?

For specific **ticket cancellation charges** applicable to tickets booked by **IRCTC agents**, please refer to the official **IRCTC website** as these charges can vary based on factors like ticket class, time of cancellation, and fare rules.

Can concessional tickets be booked from agent login?

Yes, passengers will receive all the benefits and concessions provided by IRCTC, including **concessional tickets**, on tickets booked by **IRCTC agents**, ensuring fair service for your customers.

How to locate tickets?

To locate booked tickets as an **IRCTC agent**, simply navigate to 'My Account' > 'My Transactions' > 'Booked Ticket History' on your **IRCTC agent portal**.

Do agents have a separate quota for seat booking?

No, IRCTC does not provide any separate quota specifically for **IRCTC agents** for seat booking; the general booking rules apply to all.

Do agents get priority in seat booking?

**IRCTC agents** do not receive any special priority in seat booking; the booking process is the same for everyone, ensuring fair access to tickets.

Can I book Tatkal tickets or Premium Tatkal tickets?

**IRCTC agents** are generally allowed to book **Tatkal tickets**. However, from July 1, 2025, agents will be restricted from Tatkal booking for AC class during 10:00 AM to 10:30 AM and for Non-AC class during 11:00 AM to 11:30 AM. Agents can only book after this specific window closes. Please note that **Premium Tatkal tickets** are not allowed for **IRCTC agents** to book.

Are senior citizen and other discounts provided on tickets booked by agents?

Yes, passengers will receive all applicable benefits and discounts, including those for **senior citizens** and other special categories, on **tickets booked by IRCTC agents**, ensuring passengers receive their full entitlements.

How many days in advance can I book tickets?

**IRCTC tickets** can be booked up to 150 days in advance of the travel date for all users, including **IRCTC agents**, allowing for long-term travel planning for your clients.

How much refund can I get on confirmed Tatkal tickets?

Please note that for **confirmed Tatkal tickets** booked through an **IRCTC agent**, generally no refund is provided upon cancellation, which is a standard IRCTC policy.

What type of computer is sufficient for an IRCTC ticket agent?

For operating as an **IRCTC ticket agent**, any low-configuration computer paired with a **high-speed internet** connection is generally sufficient. Which operating system can be used for IRCTC booking?

Only the **Windows operating system** is required for **IRCTC booking** through the agent portal; Apple operating systems are not directly compatible. However, an Apple computer or laptop with Windows installed (e.g., via Boot Camp or virtualization software) can be used for **IRCTC ticket booking**.
