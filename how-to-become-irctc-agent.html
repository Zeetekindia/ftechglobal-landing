How to Become an IRCTC Authorized Agent in 2025

Why Become an IRCTC Agent?

  • Legally book railway tickets for customers
  • Earn commissions on every PNR booked
  • Start your travel agency with low investment
  • High earning potential with growing railway users

Step-by-Step IRCTC Agent Registration Process

  1. Visit the official IRCTC registration or partner portals
  2. Fill the IRCTC agent registration form online
  3. Upload required documents like Aadhaar, PAN, Photo, Bank details
  4. Pay the IRCTC agent registration fees (₹1,000 – ₹5,000 approx)
  5. Receive your IRCTC Agent Login credentials within 3–7 days

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Passport size photo
  • Mobile number and Email ID
  • Bank account details

IRCTC Agent Commission & Income

IRCTC agents earn between ₹20 to ₹40 per ticket depending on booking class. With consistent bookings, monthly income can range from ₹10,000 to ₹50,000+.
