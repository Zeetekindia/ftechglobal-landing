What is an IRCTC Authorized Agent?

An IRCTC Authorized Agent is an officially appointed individual or entity empowered by IRCTC to book railway e-tickets on behalf of customers. This is distinct from a personal IRCTC user ID and comes with several advantages, including the ability to book an unlimited number of tickets, access to a dedicated IRCTC agent login portal, and the legal authority to charge a nominal service fee.

Documents Required for IRCTC Agent ID

Ensure you have the following documents ready for a smooth registration:

  • Aadhaar Card (with updated mobile number for OTP verification)
  • PAN Card
  • Recent Passport-sized Photograph
  • Valid Mobile Number (unique, not previously registered with IRCTC personal ID)
  • Valid Email ID (unique)
  • Bank Account Details (Cancelled cheque or bank passbook)
  • Shop/Office Address Proof (if applicable, e.g., electricity bill, rent agreement)

Understanding IRCTC Agent Commission & Income Potential

As an IRCTC agent, your income is primarily derived from:

  • IRCTC Commission: A fixed amount per PNR (e.g., ₹20 for SL/2S class, ₹40 for AC classes).
  • Service Charge: You can levy an additional service charge from customers, as stipulated by IRCTC.

With consistent effort and by offering value-added services, your monthly income can range significantly, potentially from ₹10,000 to ₹50,000+ or even more, depending on your location and business volume.
