Why Choose to Be an IRCTC Agent?

Becoming an IRCTC agent opens doors to a world of opportunities and significant benefits:

  • Unlimited Booking Capacity: Book an unlimited number of e-tickets, including General, Tatkal, Waiting List (WL), and RAC tickets, without restrictions. This means limitless business potential.
  • Lucrative Commission Structure: Earn high commissions on every ticket booking (₹20 for non-AC, ₹40 for AC per PNR), with potential monthly earnings exceeding ₹85,000+.
  • Low Capital Investment: Starting your IRCTC agent business requires minimal initial investment, often just a one-time application fee. No hidden charges or monthly fees.
  • Diverse Service Portfolio: Expand your offerings beyond train tickets to include flights, buses, hotels, tour packages, mobile & DTH recharges, money transfers, and insurance.
  • Flexibility & Convenience: Work at your own pace and schedule. With OTP-based login, book tickets from any device – mobile, laptop, or desktop.
  • Official Recognition & Trust: Issue authorized tickets with your agency details printed on them, building trust and credibility. No separate trade license required for railway agency services.

How to Become an IRCTC Agent: A Simple Process

The path to becoming an authorized IRCTC agent is straightforward and designed for ease:

1. Fill Online Registration Form

Complete the comprehensive IRCTC agent registration form online.

2. Pay Charges

Submit the required registration fees securely online.

3. Upload Documents

Provide necessary documents like PAN Card, Aadhaar Card (with updated mobile number and email ID), office address proof, and residential address proof. Ensure PAN/Aadhaar are not linked to personal IRCTC IDs.

4. Document Verification

Your submitted documents will undergo verification by IRCTC.

5. Receive ID & Pass

Upon successful verification, you will receive your unique IRCTC agent ID and password.

6. Successful Login & Activation

Your OTP-based login account can be activated the same day, allowing you to start booking tickets swiftly.

We provide comprehensive online training and dedicated customer support via WhatsApp, call, and email to guide you through every step.
